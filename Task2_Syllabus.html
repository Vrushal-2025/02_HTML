GEOLOGY

Syllabus

Course Overview

This course introduces the study of Earth, including the processes that shape the planet and the materials that compose it. Students will explore the fundamentals of geology, including mineralogy, petrology, tectonics, and sedimentology.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the basic concepts and principles of geology.
  • Identify various minerals, rocks, and geological structures.
  • Explore the processes that shape the Earth’s surface and interior.
  • Analyze geological maps and interpret geological data.
  • Apply geological knowledge to environmental and resource management problems.
